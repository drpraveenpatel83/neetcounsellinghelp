AACCC AYUSH Counselling 2026

Complete guide for 15% AIQ, Central Universities, National Institutes, and Deemed Universities for BAMS, BHMS, BUMS & BSMS

🌿 AACCC (Ministry of Ayush) 📅 Updated: April 2026 🎯 All India Open

Important: The Ayush Admissions Central Counseling Committee (AACCC) conducts counselling for 15% AIQ seats of Govt/Aided colleges, and 100% seats of Deemed/Central Universities and National Institutes for Under Graduate (BAMS, BSMS, BUMS, BHMS, B.Pharm-ITRA) courses.

Central Universities (BHU, AMU, etc.)100% Seats (Includes Internal + Open Quota)
National Institutes (NIA, NIH, NIUM, etc.)100% Seats

📋 Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Online Registration & Fee Payment
    Register on aaccc.gov.in. Pay the non-refundable registration fee and the refundable security deposit based on the type of colleges (AIQ vs Deemed).
  • 2
    Choice Filling & Locking
    Select courses (BAMS/BHMS/etc.) and colleges in order of preference. Ensure you lock your choices before the deadline.
  • 3
    Round 1 Allotment & Free Exit
    Seat allotment is published. Candidates can join the allotted college, or choose 'Free Exit' (not joining without losing the security deposit).
  • 4
    Round 2 & Upgradation
    Candidates can opt for upgradation from Round 1 to Round 2, and from Round 2 to Round 3. If a seat is allotted in Round 2 and not joined, the security deposit is forfeited.
  • 5
    Round 3 & Stray Vacancy
    Fresh registrations are allowed up to Round 3. Stray Vacancy fills the remaining seats (no fresh registration allowed here).

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the official website for AYUSH Counselling?

The official website for All India Quota AYUSH Counselling is aaccc.gov.in. All registrations and choice fillings are done through this portal.

What is 'Free Exit' in Round 1 of AACCC Counselling?

A 'Free Exit' means if a candidate is allotted a seat in Round 1 and chooses not to report/join the allotted college, they will not be penalized. Their refundable security deposit will remain intact, and they are eligible for Round 2.

Can I do fresh registration in Round 2 or Round 3?

Yes, fresh registrations are allowed in Round 1, Round 2, and Round 3. However, no fresh registration is permitted in the Stray Vacancy round.

Do I need to fill choices again in Round 2?

Yes. Choices filled in the previous round become null and void automatically. You must submit and lock fresh choices for every round you participate in.

What is the fee for Deemed Universities in AACCC?

To participate in Deemed Universities counselling for AYUSH, candidates must pay a total of ₹55,000 (₹5,000 Non-Refundable Registration + ₹50,000 Refundable Security Deposit).
