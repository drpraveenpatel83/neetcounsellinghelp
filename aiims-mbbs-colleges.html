📋 Service Bond, Discontinue Bond & State Quota

1. Service Bond

AIIMS MBBS students are NOT required to sign a service bond after completing the MBBS course. Students are free to pursue PG studies or work anywhere. However, state-sponsored candidates may have specific bond requirements from their respective states.

2. Discontinue Bond

If a student discontinues after admission, a discontinue bond penalty applies. The penalty typically ranges between ₹5 – ₹10 Lakh (varies by campus). This compensates AIIMS for the resources and seat opportunity utilized.

3. State Quota

AIIMS does NOT have a state quota. All MBBS seats are filled based on NEET-UG rank via MCC Central Counselling. All candidates compete in a single pool regardless of their home state.

4. Internship Stipend

AIIMS MBBS students receive a stipend during their 1-year mandatory internship after the final year.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) — AIIMS 2026

How many AIIMS are there in India in 2026?

There are 20 operational AIIMS in India as of 2026, with a total MBBS intake of 2,257 seats per year. Four more AIIMS (Awantipora, Manethi, Darbhanga, Manipur) have received Cabinet approval and are under development.

What is the annual fee at AIIMS Delhi?

The total academic fee at AIIMS Delhi is just ₹1,628 per year. Hostel charges are approximately ₹4,228 per year. This makes AIIMS one of the most affordable world-class medical institutes globally. Foreign nationals pay US $75,000 total for the course.

Does AIIMS have a 85% state quota?

No. AIIMS has no state quota. All seats are filled through NEET-UG rank via MCC Central Counselling. Any candidate from any state can apply and get admission based purely on merit rank.

Is service bond compulsory at AIIMS?

No. AIIMS MBBS students are NOT required to sign a service bond after completing the MBBS course. They are free to pursue PG (AIIMS/PGI/other), work abroad, or join any hospital without any mandatory service commitment.

What is the AIIMS MBBS discontinue bond penalty?

If a student leaves the AIIMS MBBS course midway, they must pay a discontinue bond penalty of ₹5 to ₹10 Lakh (varies by campus). This compensates AIIMS for the resources and seat opportunity used during the initial phase.

What is the stipend during AIIMS Internship?

AIIMS MBBS students receive approximately ₹30,000 per month during their 1-year compulsory internship, along with free or subsidized hostel accommodation, mess meals, and access to library and sports facilities.
