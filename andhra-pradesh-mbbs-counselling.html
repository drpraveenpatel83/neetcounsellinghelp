📌 Dr. YSR UHS conducts the State Counselling for 85% seats in Government medical colleges and 100% seats in Private medical colleges (Category A, B, and C). Deemed Universities (GITAM) are counselled by MCC.

✅ Eligibility & Seat Categories

Andhra Pradesh classifies private medical college seats into three distinct categories.

Seat CategoryEligibility Criteria
Category A (Competent Authority Quota)Strictly for AP/Telangana local Domicile candidates.
Category B (Management Quota)Open to all India. Non-domicile candidates from any state can apply.
Category C (NRI Quota)Open to NRI/NRI sponsored candidates globally.
⚠️ Candidates from Telangana are also eligible for the unreserved 15% seats of the Competent Authority Quota (Category A) in AP as per the AP Reorganisation Act.

📋 AP Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Online Registration & Fee Payment
    Register on Dr. YSR UHS portal and pay the non-refundable registration and processing fee. Candidates applying for both Cat A and Cat B must register separately.
  • 2
    State Merit List
    The university verifies uploaded documents and releases a final State Provisional Merit List. Only students in this list can fill web options.
  • 3
    Web Options Entry
    Candidates must exercise web options (Choice Filling) selecting their preferred colleges. There is no limit on the number of choices.
  • 4
    Seat Allotment & University Fee
    Upon seat allotment, candidates must pay the "University Fee" online to download the allotment letter.
  • 5
    Reporting to the College
    Report physically to the allotted medical college, submit original certificates, and pay the respective tuition fee.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Andhra Pradesh an Open State for NEET Counselling?

Yes, Andhra Pradesh is an Open State. While Category A (Govt Quota) seats are strictly for local AP/Telangana domiciles, candidates from any state across India can apply for Category B (Management Quota) and Category C (NRI Quota) seats in Private Medical Colleges.

What are Category A, B, and C seats in AP?

Category A: Competent Authority Quota seats in private colleges with subsidized fees (~₹70,000/yr) reserved for local students.
Category B: Management Quota seats (~₹13.37 Lakhs/yr) open to all India students.
Category C: NRI Quota seats (~₹30L to ₹40L/yr).

Who conducts the Andhra Pradesh MBBS Counselling 2026?

The counselling is conducted by Dr. YSR University of Health Sciences (Dr. NTRUHS), Vijayawada.
