Arunachal Pradesh AYUSH Counselling 2026

Complete protocol guide for Arunachal Pradesh APDHTE AYUSH. Check specific BAMS Central Institute dynamics, BHMS structures, and State Tribal reservations.

🌿 APDHTE Authority 📅 Updated: April 2026 🎯 AIQ & Regional Matrix

Arunachal Pradesh AYUSH Counselling is facilitated by the Directorate of Higher and Technical Education (APDHTE) for its state-sponsored quota setups, while national institutes operate dually with AACCC. For instance, the renowned NEIAFMR Pasighat strictly divides its 30 BAMS spots between an All India Quota (15 seats) and a localized North-Eastern/Arunachal quota (15 seats). Additional capacities are provided by private institutes accommodating regional BHMS demands.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who conducts the Arunachal Pradesh AYUSH Counselling 2026?

State seats are handled heavily through APDHTE (Directorate of Higher and Technical Education) while National Quotas interface via AACCC.

How many seats are reserved in NEIAFMR Pasighat for Arunachal students?

In NEIAFMR, purely 5 definitive seats are earmarked restrictively for applicants natively representing Arunachal Pradesh.

Can non-domiciles apply for AP State AYUSH seats?

The state-regulated quota aggressively relies on APST and PRC validity. Non-domiciles should strictly approach institutions via transparent 15% AIQ (AACCC) pipelines.
