State Quota (Non-APST Category II)Non-APST children of State/Central Govt employees posted in Arunachal Pradesh who have studied in the state for the last 3 years and passed Class 12 from the state.

📋 Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Online Registration
    Candidates must register online on the DHTE Arunachal Pradesh portal and upload required documents.
  • 2
    Merit List
    DHTE prepares the state merit list (Category I and Category II) based on NEET UG scores.
  • 3
    Allotment
    Since there is only one medical college in the state (TRIHMS), seat allotment is straight forward based on rank and category.
  • 4
    Reporting & Admission
    Allotted candidates must report to TRIHMS Naharlagun, complete physical document verification, and pay the requisite admission fees.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Arunachal Pradesh an Open State for NEET Counselling?

No, Arunachal Pradesh is a Closed State. Since it only has a government medical college, the state quota seats are strictly reserved for APST (Arunachal Pradesh Scheduled Tribe) candidates and eligible Category II non-APST candidates.

Who conducts the Arunachal Pradesh MBBS Counselling 2026?

The counselling is conducted by the Directorate of Higher & Technical Education (DHTE), Arunachal Pradesh.

How many medical colleges are there in Arunachal Pradesh?

Currently, there is only 1 medical college in Arunachal Pradesh: Tomo Riba Institute of Health & Medical Sciences (TRIHMS), Naharlagun, with an intake of 100 MBBS seats.
