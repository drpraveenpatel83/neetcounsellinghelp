Assam AYUSH Counselling 2026

Complete guide for Assam State Govt AYUSH seat allocations. Check specific BAMS and BHMS participation, 85% Reservation details, and Directorate regulations exclusively for students of Assam.

🌿 Directorate of AYUSH, Assam 📅 Updated: April 2026 🎯 State Quota: 85%

Assam AYUSH Counselling is predominantly administered by the Directorate of AYUSH, Government of Assam. The committee maintains the 85% state quota reserved selectively for the permanent residents of the state, filling seats in state-run BAMS and BHMS institutions. These colleges are strictly affiliated with the prestigious Srimanta Sankaradeva University of Health Sciences, Assam. Notably, Assam provides high-quality heavily subsidized government infrastructure for Homoeopathic studies, contributing exactly 150 BHMS seats combined. 15% of all remaining available Government seats fall under the AIQ AACCC counselling matrix.

📋 Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Portal Registration
    Access the Directorate of AYUSH, Assam site during the application timeframe and complete the mandatory preliminary state registration profile.
  • 2
    Document Processing & Merit Declaration
    Provide your state PRC & category details; the authority releases a verified separate state candidate merit ranking order.
  • 3
    Preference Choice Filling
    Pick among the four distinct Govt colleges depending on your degree preference (BAMS generally closes significantly earlier than BHMS ranks).
  • 4
    Seat Locking & Allotment Issue
    Confirm allotments visually and obtain the official allotment provisional document through your Dashboard.
  • 5
    Original Verification & Fee Payment
    Conclude admission strictly reporting linearly to the principal of your allocated institution alongside baseline Govt fees & original degrees.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who conducts the Assam AYUSH Counselling 2026?

The Directorate of AYUSH, Assam conducts the centralized counselling process through ayush.assam.gov.in.

Is Assam an Open State for AYUSH Counselling?

No, candidates must ideally submit permanent domicile/PRC of Assam to qualify for the broader 85% base pool limits.

What is the total number of Seats in Swahid Jadav Nath Homoeopathic Medical College?

The Swahid Jadav Nath Homoeopathic Medical College & Hospital, Guwahati has an established intake capacity of roughly 50 BHMS seats.
