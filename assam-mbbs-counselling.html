📋 Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Online Registration
    Candidates must register online on the DME Assam admission portal (dme.assam.gov.in) and pay the requisite counselling registration fee.
  • 2
    State Merit List
    DME Assam will publish a provisional state merit list of all eligible registered candidates based on their NEET UG score and Assam domicile verification.
  • 3
    Choice Filling & Locking
    Log in to submit and arrange your preferences for medical/dental colleges. Lock the choices before the deadline.
  • 4
    Allotment & College Reporting
    Download the provisional allotment letter. Report to the designated medical college for document verification, medical checkup, and admission fee payment.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Assam an Open State for NEET Counselling?

No, Assam is a Closed State. Since Assam only has Government Medical Colleges, 85% of the seats are strictly reserved for candidates holding an Assam domicile (Permanent Resident Certificate).

Who conducts the Assam MBBS Counselling 2026?

The counselling is conducted by the Directorate of Medical Education (DME), Assam.

What is the fee for Government Medical Colleges in Assam?

The annual tuition fee for Government Medical Colleges in Assam is highly subsidized, generally ranging between ₹20,000 to ₹28,000 per year depending on the institution.
