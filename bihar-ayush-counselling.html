📋 Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Online Registration
    Register yourself on the BCECEB portal by providing NEET UG details, personal info, and academic records.
  • 2
    Fee & Security Deposit Payment
    Pay the non-refundable registration fee and the refundable security deposit online (as per Govt or Private choice).
  • 3
    Merit List Publication
    Based on NEET scores, BCECEB publishes a State Rank/Merit list. Only candidates in this list proceed to choice filling.
  • 4
    Choice Filling & Locking
    Log in and fill your preferred choices of AYUSH colleges and courses. Save and lock choices before the deadline.
  • 5
    Seat Allotment & Document Verification
    Download the provisional allotment order. Report to the nodal center/allotted college for physical document verification and admission confirmation.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who conducts the Bihar AYUSH Counselling 2026?

The Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (BCECEB) conducts the UGMAC (AYUSH) counselling for BAMS, BHMS, and BUMS seats via bceceboard.bihar.gov.in.

Is Bihar an Open State for AYUSH Counselling?

Yes, Bihar allows non-domicile candidates from other states to participate and get admission into the Private AYUSH Colleges. However, Government college seats are strictly reserved for Bihar domicile candidates.

What is the fee for Govt BAMS Colleges in Bihar?

The annual fee for Govt BAMS colleges in Bihar is highly subsidized. For example, Govt Ayurvedic College Patna charges only ₹780 per year, and Rajkiya Ayodhya Shivkumar Ayurved College Begusarai charges ₹13,230 per year.
