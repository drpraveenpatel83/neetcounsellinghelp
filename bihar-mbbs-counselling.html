✅ Eligibility & Domicile Rules

CategoryCriteria Notes
85% Govt State QuotaCandidate's parents must be permanent residents of Bihar OR refugees settled in Bihar OR Govt employees posted in Bihar.
Private Medical CollegesOpen to all. Non-domicile candidates from any state can apply for Private MBBS/BDS seats in Bihar.
Basic QualificationMust have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and English. Minimum 17 years of age.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Bihar an Open State for NEET Counselling?

Yes, Bihar is an Open State. While 85% of Government college seats are strictly reserved for Bihar domicile students, candidates from any state in India can participate in the counselling to secure admission in Private Medical Colleges.

Who conducts the Bihar MBBS Counselling 2026?

The counselling is conducted by the Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (BCECEB) through the UGMAC (Under Graduate Medical Admission Counselling) process on the portal bceceboard.bihar.gov.in.

What is the security deposit for Private Colleges in Bihar?

Candidates applying for Private Medical Colleges in Bihar must deposit a refundable security amount of ₹2,00,000 during registration.
