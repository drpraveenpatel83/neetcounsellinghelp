📋 Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Online Registration & Fee Payment
    Candidates must register online on the DME Chhattisgarh admission portal (cgdme.co.in) and pay the counselling registration fee.
  • 2
    Security Deposit
    Pay the refundable security deposit online (Govt vs Private) to become eligible for choice filling.
  • 3
    State Merit List
    DME CG will publish a provisional state merit list of all eligible registered candidates based on their NEET UG score.
  • 4
    Choice Filling & Locking
    Log in to submit and lock your preferences for medical colleges.
  • 5
    Allotment & College Reporting
    Download the provisional allotment letter, undergo physical scrutiny of original documents at the allotted college, and pay the required admission fee.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Chhattisgarh an Open State for NEET Counselling?

Yes, Chhattisgarh is an Open State. While 85% of Government college seats are strictly reserved for Chhattisgarh domicile students, candidates from any state in India can participate in the counselling to secure admission in the Management Quota seats of Private Medical Colleges.

Who conducts the Chhattisgarh MBBS Counselling 2026?

The counselling is conducted by the Directorate of Medical Education (DME), Chhattisgarh.

What is the fee for Government Medical Colleges in Chhattisgarh?

The annual fee for pure Government Medical Colleges in Chhattisgarh is highly subsidized, ranging between ₹34,500 and ₹57,000 per year depending on the institution.
