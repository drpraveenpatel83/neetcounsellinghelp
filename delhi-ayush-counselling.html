Delhi has a dual-counselling system for its 85% AYUSH state quota seats depending on the university affiliation.

📌 DU (FMSC) handles 85% state quota for Ayurveda and Unani Tibbia College, and Nehru Homoeopathic Medical College. GGSIPU handles 85% state quota for CBPACS (BAMS) and Dr. B.R. Sur Homoeopathic College.

✅ Eligibility & Delhi Quota Rules

CategoryCriteria Notes
85% Delhi State QuotaCandidate must have passed 11th and 12th standard (or equivalent) from a recognized school/institution situated within the National Capital Territory (NCT) of Delhi.
Patrachar / NIOS StudentsEligible if both their study center and examination center were located within the NCT of Delhi. A certificate from Patrachar/NIOS authority is required.
15% All India QuotaOpen to all candidates across India via AACCC (Ayush Admissions Central Counseling Committee) counselling.
Basic QualificationPassed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotech, and English. Minimum 50% for UR, 40% for SC/ST, and 45% for PwD. NEET UG 2026 qualified.
⚠️ Delhi does not issue traditional domicile certificates for admissions. The state quota is purely based on the location of your 11th and 12th passing school (Must be inside Delhi).

📋 DU (FMSC) Counselling Process

  • 1
    Online Registration & Fee Payment
    Visit fmsc.ac.in. Register using NEET roll number and DOB. Pay the registration fee (UR/OBC/EWS: ₹1,500; SC/ST/PwD: ₹1,000).
  • 2
    Document Upload & Verification
    Upload NEET scorecard, 10th/12th certificates, Delhi school principal certificate, and category certificates. Trained staff will verify these online.
  • 3
    Choice Filling & Seat Allotment
    After successful verification, fill choices for Tibbia or Nehru Homoeopathic. Check the allotment result online.

📋 GGSIPU (IPU) Counselling Process

  • 1
    Enrolment & Application Fee
    Visit ipu.admissions.nic.in. Fresh applicants must pay an application fee of ₹2,500 (if not paid earlier) plus a Counselling Participation Fee of ₹1,000.
  • 2
    Choice Filling & Allotment
    Fill preferences for CBPACS or Dr. B.R. Sur. Seats are allocated based on merit and category.
  • 3
    Part Academic Fee Payment
    To confirm the allotted seat, candidates must pay a Part Academic Fee of ₹60,000. Failing this, the seat is canceled, and the candidate cannot participate in subsequent rounds.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who conducts the Delhi AYUSH Counselling 2026?

Delhi has two counselling authorities for AYUSH. Delhi University (FMSC) conducts counselling for Tibbia College and Nehru Homoeopathic College. Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) conducts counselling for CBPACS and Dr. B.R. Sur Homoeopathic College.

What is the eligibility for Delhi 85% State Quota in AYUSH?

To be eligible for the 85% Delhi State Quota, a candidate must have completed their 11th and 12th standard from a recognized school situated within the National Capital Territory (NCT) of Delhi.

Can I participate in both DU and IPU AYUSH counselling?

Yes, if you meet the 85% Delhi quota eligibility (11th & 12th from Delhi), you can register and participate in both DU and IPU counselling simultaneously by paying their respective registration fees.
