📌 MCC handles 85% state quota for DU colleges (MAMC, LHMC, UCMS) and Central Institutes (VMMC, ABVIMS RML). GGSIPU handles 85% state quota for its affiliated state colleges (BSA, NDMC, ACMS).

✅ Eligibility & Domicile Rules

CategoryCriteria Notes
85% Delhi State Quota (DU / IPU)Candidate must have passed 11th and 12th standard from a recognized school located within the National Capital Territory (NCT) of Delhi.
15% All India QuotaOpen to all candidates across India via MCC counselling.
Army College of Medical Sciences (ACMS)Reserved strictly for wards of eligible serving Army personnel, Ex-Servicemen, and war widows. Not for civilian general public.
⚠️ Delhi does not issue traditional domicile certificates for MBBS. The criteria is purely based on the location of your 11th and 12th passing school (Must be inside Delhi).

📋 DU MBBS/BDS Counselling & FMSc Registration Process

Candidates allotted a seat under Delhi University (DU) 85% State Quota or 15% AIQ must register with the Faculty of Medical Sciences (FMSc) at fmsc.du.ac.in.

FMSc Registration Fee (Non-Refundable)

CategoryRegistration Fee
General / OBC / EWS₹1,500
SC / ST / PwBD₹1,000

Step-by-Step Admission Process

  • 1
    MCC Choice Filling & Seat Allotment
    Register on the MCC portal, fill preferred colleges, and lock your choices. Seat allotment results are released in PDF format for each round (Round 1, Round 2, Mop-up).
  • 2
    FMSc Portal Registration
    Visit fmsc.du.ac.in. Enter your NEET roll number, email, mobile, and DOB. A system-generated OTP will be sent to your mobile, which will act as your password.
  • 3
    Fill Application & Upload Documents
    Log in using your roll number and OTP. Download your NEET scorecard, then fill in your personal, family, and academic (Class 10, 12, NEET) details. Upload self-attested document copies, photo, and signature.
  • 4
    Fee Payment & Final Submission
    Cross-check all entered details, pay the FMSc application fee (₹1,500 or ₹1,000) online, submit the form, and take a printout of the confirmation page.
  • 5
    Reporting to the College
    Complete your document verification at the Faculty of Medical Sciences (DU), and then report to your designated medical college to join the MBBS/BDS course.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Delhi an Open State for NEET Counselling?

No, for the 85% State Quota, Delhi is a Closed State. You must have passed 11th and 12th from a school in Delhi NCT to be eligible for state seats in DU and IPU.

Who conducts the Delhi MBBS Counselling 2026?

Delhi has two authorities: MCC handles the 85% state quota for Delhi University (DU) colleges and Central Institutes. GGSIPU handles the 85% state quota for its affiliated IPU colleges.

What is the fee for Government Medical Colleges in Delhi?

Fees vary. DU colleges (like MAMC, LHMC) are highly subsidized, charging around ₹1,360 to ₹5,300 per year. IPU colleges charge between ₹27,500 and ₹76,000 approximately per year.
