Goa AYUSH Counselling 2026

Complete planning guide for Goa AYUSH UG admission. Check registration process, available Seat Matrix, and BAMS/BHMS participating colleges.

🌿 DTE Goa 📅 Updated: April 2026 🎯 State Quota: 85%

Goa AYUSH Counselling is administered by the Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), Goa, covering state quota seats in AYUSH Colleges. BAMS and BHMS admissions for state residents are processed through the DTE common merit list based on NEET execution. Candidates need to register on the official portal and select their preferences based on the available seat matrix and applicable reservation rosters. The remaining 15% of seats in participating colleges are managed by the AYUSH Admissions Central Counseling Committee (AACCC) under the All India Quota.

📋 Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Register online on DTE Portal
    Fill out 'Form A' (or the equivalent official AYUSH admission form) via the designated payment link during the registration window.
  • 2
    Submit Application & Document Review
    Submit the application alongside NEET scorecards, HSSC passing certificates, and Residence Proof.
  • 3
    Check the merit list
    Review the category and rank details declared by DTE Goa.
  • 4
    Fill and Lock Choices
    Prioritise the available BAMS and BHMS colleges. Since the seat pool is limited (210 seats max), arrangement is crucial.
  • 5
    Seat Allotment & Admission
    Secure the admission by downloading the allotment letter and reporting to the institution with necessary tuition fee demand drafts.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who conducts the Goa AYUSH Counselling 2026?

The Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), Goa conducts the AYUSH counselling for the 85% state quota BAMS and BHMS seats.

What is the total number of BAMS seats in Goa?

There are approximately 160 BAMS seats available across Goa, including 100 Government seats and 60 Private seats.

What is the Academic Fees for Govt Ayurveda Colleges in Goa?

The academic fee for Government/Grant-in-aid Ayurveda colleges can be roughly ₹55,400 per year, subject to updates by DTE Goa.
