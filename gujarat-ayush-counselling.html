✅ Eligibility Criteria & Domicile Rules

CategoryCriteria Notes
State Quota (Gujarat)Must have passed 12th (B/AB Group) with PCB from Gujarat Board / CBSE / ISCE / NIOS schools located in Gujarat. Qualified NEET UG 2026. Must be a Domicile of Gujarat.
Special Categories (State Quota)Children of Gujarat Govt employees posted outside, defense personnel's children, NRI/OCI born in Gujarat, and Navodaya Vidyalaya students (Std 1-8 in Gujarat) are eligible.
15% AIQ in SFI BAMS & BHMSPassed 12th from any Indian board. Qualified NEET UG 2026. Must apply separately via the "AIO SFI AYUSH" link on the portal.
⚠️ Important: Candidates appearing for Supplementary exams of 2026 are NOT eligible for AYUSH counselling in the same year.

📋 Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Online PIN Purchase & Registration Fee
    Candidates must purchase a PIN online via medadmgujarat.org by paying the total initial fee (₹11,000).
  • 2
    Online Registration
    Fill the online registration form using the purchased PIN and NEET UG 2026 details.
  • 3
    Document Verification at Help Center
    Document verification is mandatory. Candidates must physically visit the allotted Help Centers with original documents and self-attested copies to get verified.
  • 4
    Merit List & Choice Filling
    A state merit list is published. Eligible candidates then select and lock their preferred colleges and courses.
  • 5
    Seat Allotment & Admission
    Seats are allotted based on merit. Candidates must pay the tuition fee and report to the designated center/college for admission.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who conducts the Gujarat AYUSH Counselling 2026?

The Admission Committee for Professional Under Graduate Medical Educational Courses (ACPUGMEC), Gujarat conducts the counselling via medadmgujarat.org.

Can other state students apply for Gujarat AYUSH Counselling?

Yes, non-domicile candidates can apply for the 15% All India Quota (AIQ) in Self-Financed Institutes (SFI) BAMS & BHMS seats through a separate registration link on the ACPUGMEC portal.

What is the registration fee for Gujarat AYUSH Counselling?

Candidates must pay a non-refundable PIN fee of ₹1,000 and a refundable security deposit of ₹10,000, making the total initial payment ₹11,000.
