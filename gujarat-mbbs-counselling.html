📋 Counselling Process

  • 1
    Online PIN Purchase & Registration
    Candidates must purchase a 14-digit PIN online from medadmgujarat.org and complete the registration form using NEET Roll No.
  • 2
    Document Verification
    After online registration, candidates must visit designated Help Centers for physical verification of original documents and get a verification slip.
  • 3
    State Merit List
    ACPUGMEC publishes the state merit list of all verified candidates.
  • 4
    Choice Filling & Locking
    Candidates log in to fill their choices of colleges and quotas (Govt, Management, NRI). Save and lock choices before the deadline.
  • 5
    Seat Allotment & Reporting
    Download the allotment letter, pay the tuition fee online or at a designated bank, and report to the Help Center/College to submit original documents.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Gujarat an Open State for NEET Counselling?

No, Gujarat is a Closed State. Candidates from other states cannot participate in the 85% state quota counselling for Govt or GMERS colleges. However, they are eligible for the Management/NRI quota seats in Private Medical Colleges.

Who conducts the Gujarat MBBS Counselling 2026?

The counselling is conducted by the Admission Committee for Professional Under Graduate Medical Educational Courses (ACPUGMEC), Gujarat through the official portal medadmgujarat.org.

What is GMERS and what is its fee structure?

GMERS (Gujarat Medical Education and Research Society) colleges are semi-government institutions. They offer State Quota seats (approx ₹3.00 Lakhs/year) and Management Quota seats (approx ₹8.25 Lakhs/year).

What is the annual fee for Government Medical Colleges in Gujarat?

The annual fee for pure Government Medical Colleges (like B.J. Medical College, GMC Surat) is fixed at ₹25,000 per year.
