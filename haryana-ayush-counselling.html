📋 Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Online Registration
    Register on the SKAU Haryana counselling portal. Fill in personal details, NEET scores, and upload necessary certificates.
  • 2
    Fee Payment
    Pay the non-refundable registration fee online according to your category.
  • 3
    Document Verification
    Online/Offline document verification for state domicile, category certificates, and NEET credentials.
  • 4
    Choice Filling & Locking
    Select and arrange your preferred Govt. and Private AYUSH colleges. Lock choices before the deadline.
  • 5
    Seat Allotment & Admission
    Check the merit list and seat allotment result. Download the allotment letter and report to the allotted college to deposit the tuition fee.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Can non-Haryana students apply for Haryana AYUSH Counselling?

Yes, Haryana is an Open State for Management category seats in Private AYUSH Colleges. Candidates from All Over India are eligible to apply irrespective of their State of domicile.

Who conducts the Haryana AYUSH Counselling?

The counselling is conducted by Shri Krishna AYUSH University (SKAU), Kurukshetra on behalf of the Directorate of AYUSH, Haryana.

What is the fee for Private BAMS Colleges in Haryana?

The standard fee for Private BAMS colleges affiliated with SKAU is ₹2.40 Lakhs per annum (with a 5% annual increase). However, Private University colleges like SGT University and Baba Mastnath University charge between ₹3.90 Lakhs and ₹4.75 Lakhs per annum.
