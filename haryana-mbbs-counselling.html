📋 Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Online Registration & Fee Payment
    Candidates must register online on the DMER/UHSR admission portal and pay the non-refundable registration fee.
  • 2
    Security Deposit
    Pay the requisite refundable security deposit online (Govt vs Private) to become eligible for choice filling.
  • 3
    State Merit List
    A provisional combined merit list of all registered candidates will be published based on NEET UG ranks.
  • 4
    Choice Filling & Locking
    Log in to submit and lock your preferences for medical/dental colleges.
  • 5
    Allotment & College Reporting
    Download the provisional allotment letter, pay the required tuition fee online (via admission portal), and report to the designated nodal center/college for document verification and medical checkup.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Haryana an Open State for NEET Counselling?

Yes, Haryana is an Open State. While 85% of Government college seats are strictly reserved for Haryana domicile students, candidates from any state in India can participate in the counselling to secure admission in the Management Quota seats of Private Medical Colleges.

Who conducts the Haryana MBBS Counselling 2026?

The counselling is conducted by the Department of Medical Education and Research (DMER), Haryana and Pt. B.D. Sharma University of Health Sciences, Rohtak.

What is the fee for Government Medical Colleges in Haryana?

The annual fee for pure Government Medical Colleges in Haryana (like PGIMS Rohtak) is approximately ₹52,070 per year. Candidates must also be aware of the state's bond policy.
