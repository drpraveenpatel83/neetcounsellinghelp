Himachal Pradesh AYUSH Counselling 2026

Complete planning guide for HP AYUSH UG admission. Check registration process, available Seat Matrix, and BAMS/BHMS/BNYS participating colleges via AMRU.

🌿 AMRU Himachal Pradesh 📅 Updated: April 2026 🎯 State Quota: 85%

Himachal Pradesh AYUSH Counselling is organized uniformly by Atal Medical & Research University (AMRU), Himachal Pradesh on behalf of the Department of AYUSH. The central AMRU portal covers 85% of seats inside the single state Government College and 100% seats in Private AYUSH institutions under the State Quota. Candidates from across Himachal Pradesh can compete for BAMS, BHMS, and BNYS seats via the state rank list generated directly from their verified NEET UG score. Note that the remaining 15% seats in HP Govt colleges are handled centrally by AACCC.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who conducts the HP AYUSH Counselling 2026?

The Atal Medical & Research University (AMRU), Himachal Pradesh typically manages the common counselling portal.

Is HP an Open State for AYUSH Counselling?

HP generally reserves its State Quota for native bona fide residents through strict document verifications, but non-domiciles may apply for the limited private institution AIQ structures where applicable.

What is the fee for Private BAMS Colleges in Himachal Pradesh?

The tuition fee generally scales between ₹2,50,000 to ₹3,00,000 per year depending on whether the seat is taken under State Quota or Management AIQ Quota.
