📋 Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Online Registration & Fee Payment
    Candidates must register online on the AMRU admission portal and pay the non-refundable registration fee.
  • 2
    State Merit List
    AMRU will publish a provisional state merit list of all eligible registered candidates based on NEET UG ranks and verified categories.
  • 3
    Choice Filling & Locking
    Log in to submit and arrange your preferences for medical/dental colleges. Lock your choices before the deadline.
  • 4
    Seat Allotment
    The seat allotment result will be published online. Download your provisional allotment letter.
  • 5
    Reporting & Medical Examination
    Report to the allotted college with all original documents for verification, complete the medical examination, and deposit the required tuition fee.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Himachal Pradesh an Open State for NEET Counselling?

Yes, Himachal Pradesh is an Open State. Non-domicile candidates from other states can apply for the Management Quota seats in Private Medical Colleges like MMU Solan. However, 85% of Government college seats and State Quota private seats are strictly for Bonafide Himachali candidates.

Who conducts the Himachal Pradesh MBBS Counselling 2026?

The counselling is conducted by Atal Medical and Research University (AMRU), Mandi on behalf of the Department of Medical Education & Research, HP.

What is the fee for Government Medical Colleges in Himachal Pradesh?

The total fee for the 1st year in Government Medical Colleges is ₹60,000. For the 2nd, 3rd, 4th, and 5th years, it is ₹50,000 per year.
