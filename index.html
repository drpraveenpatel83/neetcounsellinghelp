About Counselling

What is NEET Counselling?

NEET counselling is the official seat allotment process for MBBS, BDS, and other medical courses across India based on your NEET-UG score.

MCC Counselling

Medical Counselling Committee conducts counselling for 15% All India Quota seats in government colleges and all deemed/central university seats.

State Counselling

Each state conducts its own counselling for 85% State Quota seats. You must register separately with your state's counselling authority.

Timeline Matters

Missing a round can cost you your preferred seat. Multiple rounds of counselling take place — stay updated with official MCC and state schedules.

Documents Ready

Keep all documents verified and ready — NEET scorecard, Class 10 & 12 certificates, ID proof, category certificates, and domicile proof.

i

Important: NEET counselling has strict deadlines. Missing registration windows or document submission can result in loss of admission opportunity even with a good score.

Step-by-Step Guide

How Counselling Works

Follow these steps carefully to secure your medical seat

01
MCC

Register on MCC Portal

Visit mcc.nic.in and complete your registration using NEET roll number, date of birth, and mobile number. Pay the registration and security fee online.

  • Create account on mcc.nic.in
  • Fill personal and academic details
  • Pay registration fee (Rs. 1000 for General / Rs. 500 for SC/ST)
  • Pay security deposit (Rs. 10,000 / Rs. 5,000)
02
Choice

Fill Choice of Colleges

Browse and select your preferred colleges and courses in order of priority. You can add, remove, and rearrange choices before locking.

  • Research colleges, fees, and cutoffs
  • Add colleges in order of preference
  • Consider location, infrastructure, faculty
  • Lock choices before the deadline
03
Allotment

Seat Allotment Result

MCC processes all choices and NEET ranks to allot seats. Check your allotment letter on the portal. Three options are available on allotment.

  • Accept & join (forfeit upgrades)
  • Accept & wait for upgrade (participate in next round)
  • Reject allotment (continue for better options)
04
Report

Report to College

If you accept the seat, report to the allotted college within the specified deadline for document verification and fee payment.

  • Download allotment letter
  • Carry all original documents
  • Pay first-year tuition fee
  • Complete college enrollment formalities
05
State

Participate in State Counselling

Simultaneously register for your home state counselling for 85% state quota seats. State counselling runs in parallel with MCC rounds.

  • Register on state counselling portal
  • Submit domicile and category certificates
  • Fill state college preferences
  • Attend document verification if called

Counselling Rounds Overview

Round 1

First allotment based on filled choices and NEET rank

Round 2

Upgrade or fresh allotment for students who opted to wait

Mop-Up Round

For vacant seats after Rounds 1 and 2 (Deemed/Central only)

Stray Vacancy

Final round for any remaining seats after all previous rounds

Be Prepared

Documents Checklist

Keep these documents ready before counselling begins

Academic Documents

  • NEET-UG Admit Card
  • NEET-UG Result / Scorecard
  • Class 10 Mark Sheet & Certificate
  • Class 12 Mark Sheet & Certificate
  • Class 12 Passing Certificate

Identity Documents

  • Aadhar Card
  • Passport (if applicable)
  • Passport Size Photographs (10+)
  • Birth Certificate

Category / Special Documents

  • Caste Certificate (OBC/SC/ST)
  • EWS Certificate (if applicable)
  • PwD Certificate (if applicable)
  • Domicile / Residence Certificate
  • Migration Certificate

Financial Documents

  • Bank Account Details
  • Net Banking / UPI / Debit Card
  • Income Certificate (for EWS/scholarship)
Tip

Make multiple photocopies of all documents and keep them in a dedicated folder. Also maintain digital scans in a cloud storage for quick access.
