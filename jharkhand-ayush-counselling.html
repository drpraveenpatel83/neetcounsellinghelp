Jharkhand AYUSH Counselling 2026

Complete guide for BAMS & BHMS admissions. Check eligibility, deposit details, comprehensive college-wise fee structure, and seat matrix.

🌿 State Counselling Authority (DHTE) 📅 Updated: April 2026 🎯 State Quota: 85%

Jharkhand AYUSH Counselling is administered by the State Counselling Authority (DHTE), covering 85% of the seats in Government Colleges and 100% of the seats in Private Colleges under the State Quota. The remaining 15% of seats in Government Colleges are managed by the AYUSH Admissions Central Counseling Committee (AACCC) under the All India Quota. The counselling process for both categories is distinct. For Jharkhand AYUSH Counselling under the State Quota, candidates must follow the guidelines and schedule set by the State Counselling Authority. Meanwhile, for the AACCC AYUSH Counselling under the All India Quota, candidates need to adhere to the procedures established by the AACCC, ensuring a transparent and systematic allocation of seats for AYUSH courses.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who conducts the Jharkhand AYUSH Counselling 2026?

The State Counselling Authority (DHTE) conducts the AYUSH counselling for BAMS and BHMS seats.

Is Jharkhand an Open State for AYUSH Counselling?

Jharkhand is primarily a Closed State. State quota seats and most private seats are prioritized for local domiciles. However, vacant management seats in private colleges may be opened to non-domiciles in later rounds.

What is the fee for Private BAMS Colleges in Jharkhand?

The annual fee for Private BAMS colleges like Surya Mukhi Dinesh Ayurvedic Medical College is approximately ₹2,50,000 per year.
