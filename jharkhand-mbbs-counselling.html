📋 Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Online Registration & Fee Payment
    Candidates must register online on the JCECEB admission portal and pay the application fee (₹1000 for Gen/EWS/BC, ₹500 for SC/ST/Females).
  • 2
    State Merit List
    JCECEB will publish a provisional state merit list of all eligible registered candidates based on their NEET UG score and state domicile.
  • 3
    Choice Filling & Locking
    Log in to submit and arrange your preferences for medical/dental colleges. Pay the counselling/security fee if applicable before locking.
  • 4
    Allotment & College Reporting
    Download the provisional allotment letter and report to the designated college for document verification, medical checkup, and fee payment.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Jharkhand an Open State for NEET Counselling?

Jharkhand is primarily a Closed State, meaning state quota seats and most private seats are prioritized for local domiciles. However, vacant management seats in private colleges may be opened to non-domiciles in later rounds.

Who conducts the Jharkhand MBBS Counselling 2026?

The counselling is conducted by the Jharkhand Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (JCECEB).

What is the fee for Manipal TATA Medical College under State Quota?

For Jharkhand domicile candidates, Manipal TATA charges ₹12,06,750 per year for UR candidates, and a subsidized fee of ₹8,04,500 per year for SC/ST/BC/EWS candidates. Its AIQ seats cost ₹16.09 Lakh/yr via MCC.
