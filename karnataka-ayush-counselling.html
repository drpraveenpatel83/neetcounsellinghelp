✅ Eligibility Criteria & Non-Karnataka Rules

CriteriaDetails
Karnataka CandidatesEligible for 85% Government quota seats and reservation benefits. Must have valid domicile or satisfy KEA schooling clauses.
Non-Karnataka CandidatesOpen State Eligibility: Non-Karnataka candidates are eligible ONLY for the notified open/management seats in Private AYUSH Colleges.
NEET Percentile for Non-KarnatakaMust have secured minimum 50th percentile (UR category) to register. SC/ST/OBC cutoff criteria apply to Karnataka reserved candidates only.
Reservation for Non-KarnatakaNot eligible for any reservation (SC/ST/OBC). Considered under General Merit.
Document VerificationNon-Karnataka candidates who register with KEA do NOT need to appear for physical document verification in Karnataka.

📋 Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Online Registration & Fee Payment
    Register on the KEA portal during the notified window and pay the application fee online.
  • 2
    Document Verification
    Karnataka domicile candidates must attend document verification at designated nodal centers. Non-Karnataka candidates are exempt.
  • 3
    Option Entry (Choice Filling)
    Eligible candidates log in and fill their preferred colleges and courses in priority order.
  • 4
    Mock Allotment & Real Allotment
    A mock allotment is released to give an idea, followed by an option editing window. Then the real Round 1 seat allotment is published.
  • 5
    Admission & Reporting
    Download the KEA admission order, pay the tuition fees via challan/online, and report to the allotted college.

📁 Documents Required for Admission

Candidates allotted a seat in BAMS, BHMS, or BUMS colleges must carry originals + one attested copy of the following:

NEET-UG 2025 Admit Card (NTA)
NEET-UG 2025 Result / Rank Letter
Class 10 Certificate (DOB Proof)
Class 12 (10+2) Marks Sheet & Certificate
8 Passport size photographs
Provisional Allotment Letter (KEA)
Govt ID Proof (Aadhaar/PAN/Voter ID)
Medical Fitness Certificate
Category Certificates (SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwD) - If applicable

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Can non-Karnataka students apply for Karnataka AYUSH Counselling?

Yes, non-Karnataka candidates are eligible, but only for the notified Open/Management Quota seats in Private Colleges. They cannot claim any reservation, must have 50th percentile (UR) in NEET, and do not need to attend physical document verification in Karnataka.

Who conducts the Karnataka AYUSH Counselling 2026?

The Karnataka Examination Authority (KEA) conducts the counselling for all BAMS, BHMS, BUMS, and BNYS seats in Government and Private colleges across Karnataka state.

What is the fee for Govt BAMS Colleges in Karnataka?

The annual tuition fee for Government AYUSH (BAMS/BHMS/BUMS) colleges in Karnataka generally ranges between ₹15,000 and ₹25,000 per year.
