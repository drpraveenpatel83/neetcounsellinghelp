📋 Counselling Registration Process

  • 1
    Online Registration
    Visit the official website (kea.kar.nic.in), click on the registration link, and fill in your personal details, category, and NEET score.
  • 2
    Upload Documents & Images
    Upload required documents including a recent color photograph, signature, and category certificates as a single PDF.
  • 3
    Declaration & Submission
    Review all information, accept the declaration, and submit. An application number will be generated.
  • 4
    Application Fee Payment
    Pay the registration fee of ₹2,500/- via internet banking, debit/credit card. Save the payment slip.
  • 5
    Document Verification & Option Entry
    After successful verification, eligible candidates can proceed to 'Option Entry' (Choice Filling) on the KEA portal.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Karnataka an Open State for NEET Counselling?

Yes, Karnataka is an Open State. Non-domicile candidates from other states can apply for the Open/Management Quota seats in Private Medical Colleges. However, Govt quota seats are strictly for Karnataka domiciles.

Who conducts the Karnataka MBBS Counselling 2026?

The counselling is conducted by the Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA) through the official portal kea.kar.nic.in.

What is the HK Quota (Article 371J)?

Candidates belonging to the Hyderabad-Karnataka region have special reservation under Article 371(J). They must possess a valid eligibility certificate to claim this reservation during state counselling.

What is the annual fee for Government Medical Colleges in Karnataka?

The annual tuition fee for pure Government Medical Colleges in Karnataka is approximately ₹65,100 per year. For ESIC colleges, it is ₹1,10,100 per year.
