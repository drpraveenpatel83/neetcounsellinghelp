💳 Counselling Registration Fee & Security Deposit

To participate in the Maharashtra NEET UG counselling, candidates must pay a non-refundable online registration fee. Maharashtra CET Cell does not collect a refundable security deposit online.

Quota / Seat TypeRegistration Fee (Non-Refundable)Security Deposit (Refundable)
State Quota (85%)₹1,000N/A (Paid directly to college)
Institutional / NRI Quota (15%)₹5,000N/A (Paid directly to college)
Both (State + NRI)₹6,000N/A (Paid directly to college)
📌 Important: Tuition fees and deposits are paid via Demand Draft (DD) at the time of physical reporting to the allotted college. If a candidate leaves an allotted seat after the cut-off date, a penalty of ₹1,00,000 is levied.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Maharashtra an Open State for NEET Counselling?

No, Maharashtra is a Closed State. Candidates from other states cannot participate in the 85% state quota counselling for Govt or Private colleges. They are only eligible for the 15% NRI/Institutional quota in private medical colleges.

Who conducts the Maharashtra MBBS Counselling 2026?

The counselling is conducted by the State Common Entrance Test Cell (State CET Cell), Maharashtra through the official portal cetcell.mahacet.org.

Are Deemed Universities in Maharashtra covered under State Counselling?

No, Deemed Universities like DY Patil, MGM, Symbiosis, and KIMS Karad are completely out of state counselling scope. Their 100% seats are allotted through MCC AIQ counselling.

What is the annual fee for Government Medical Colleges in Maharashtra?

The annual fee for Govt Medical Colleges is fixed at ₹78,000 (Tuition Fee) + ₹5,000 (Development Fee). Total expenses including hostel and library come to approx ₹90,000 per year.
