Manipur AYUSH Counselling 2026

Important details detailing the state medical nomination processes due to the absence of internal BAMS/BHMS constituent entities within the state borders.

🌿 Directorate of AYUSH, Manipur 🎯 Non-Institutional Nomination Model

Urgent Notice: Currently, there are zero operational Government or Private BAMS/BHMS colleges physically sited within Manipur. Because of this, standard internal state counseling matrices do not exist. Instead, eligible domiciled students participate in central external allocation arrays via AACCC or petition the Directorate of AYUSH, Govt of Manipur for distinct state-led "Nomination" seats sponsored externally across the rest of the nation.

📌 How Manipur Students Can Pursue AYUSH

  • 1
    Government Nomad / External Nominations
    The Directorate of AYUSH (mn.gov.in) conditionally invites applications from domiciled NEET qualified candidates. Through this vector, top candidates are effectively nominated using state quotas into government colleges located elsewhere in India. Keep notifications continuously monitored during counselling seasons.
  • 2
    AACCC All India Quota
    Independently register natively on the purely central portal (aaccc.gov.in) to contest freely against generic General or Category limits without relying on state-sponsorship.
