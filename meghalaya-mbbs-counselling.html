📋 Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Application Submission
    Candidates must apply via the specific portal or offline forms issued by the Department of Health & Family Welfare, Meghalaya or NEIGRIHMS.
  • 2
    Screening & Verification
    Submit all original domicile certificates, tribal certificates, and NEET scorecards for verification.
  • 3
    State Merit List
    A combined merit list is published for eligible state candidates based on NEET UG scores.
  • 4
    Seat Allotment & Nomination
    The state government nominates students against the state quota seats available in NEIGRIHMS or outside the state (central pool). Private seats are allotted directly via their respective university processes.
  • 5
    Admission & Bond Submission
    Report to the allotted medical college, sign the mandatory service bond (if applicable), and pay the admission fees.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Meghalaya an Open State for NEET Counselling?

Meghalaya is a Closed State for Government seats. However, Non-domicile candidates from other states can participate and get admission into the Management Quota seats of Private Medical Colleges like PA Sangma International Medical College.

Who conducts the Meghalaya MBBS Counselling 2026?

The state quota counselling and candidate nomination is conducted by the Department of Health and Family Welfare, Government of Meghalaya, while NEIGRIHMS manages its own specific North East open seat counselling.

What is the fee and bond structure for NEIGRIHMS Shillong?

The annual fee (including hostel) for NEIGRIHMS is highly subsidized at ₹16,600. In the first year, candidates also pay ₹11,000 as one-time fees and a ₹20,000 refundable security deposit. There is a mandatory ₹10 Lakh service bond.
