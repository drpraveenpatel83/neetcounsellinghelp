✅ Eligibility Criteria & Domicile Rules

CriteriaDetails
NEET QualificationMust be NEET UG 2026 qualified. General: 50th Percentile | SC/ST/OBC: 40th Percentile.
Educational Qualification10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology (PCB) and English.
State Domicile (Govt Quota)Candidates must be domiciled in Madhya Pradesh to be eligible for Government College seats.
Private Colleges (Other States)Non-domicile candidates can register, but they are given lowest priority. They may be considered if seats remain vacant in Mop-up rounds.

📋 Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Online Registration & Fee Payment
    Register on the ayush.mponline.gov.in portal. Pay the non-refundable registration fee of ₹500 online.
  • 2
    Document Verification
    Candidates must complete the document verification process as notified by the authority.
  • 3
    State Merit List
    A merit list of verified candidates will be published based on NEET scores.
  • 4
    Choice Filling & Locking
    Eligible candidates log in to select their preferred colleges and courses. Lock the choices before the deadline.
  • 5
    Seat Allotment & Reporting
    Download the allotment letter and report to the designated college for final admission and fee payment.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who conducts the Madhya Pradesh AYUSH Counselling 2026?

The Department of AYUSH, Government of Madhya Pradesh conducts the counselling via the MP Online portal (ayush.mponline.gov.in).

What is the registration and security fee for MP AYUSH Counselling?

The registration fee is ₹500. The refundable security money is ₹15,000 for Unreserved Category and ₹5,000 for Reserved Category candidates.

What is the fee for Govt BAMS Colleges in Madhya Pradesh?

The annual fee for Government BAMS colleges in MP ranges from ₹40,000 to approximately ₹1,02,735 depending on the specific institution.
