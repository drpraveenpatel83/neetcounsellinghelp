📋 Counselling Process

  • 1
    Online Profile Creation & Registration
    Register on dme.mponline.gov.in using NEET Roll No. and generate a profile. Pay the portal fee (approx ₹1,000) to complete registration.
  • 2
    State Merit List Publication
    DME MP releases a state merit list of all registered candidates containing their State Rank.
  • 3
    Choice Filling & Locking
    Login to your profile and fill the preferences of Medical/Dental colleges. Save and lock choices before the deadline.
  • 4
    Seat Allotment Result
    First round allotment is published online. Download the allotment letter if a seat is allocated.
  • 5
    Document Verification & Admission
    Report to the allotted medical college with original documents, submit the bond, and pay the required fees to secure admission.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Madhya Pradesh an Open State for NEET Counselling?

MP is a closed state for Government Colleges. For Private Colleges, non-domicile candidates can register, but they are given lowest priority. Seats are allotted to other state candidates only if there are vacant seats after exhausting all eligible MP domicile candidates.

What is Mukhyamantri Medhavi Vidyarthi Yojna (MMVY)?

MMVY is a scholarship scheme by the MP Government where the entire tuition fee for MBBS (both Govt and Private) is paid by the state if the student meets certain criteria (like >70% in MP Board or >85% in CBSE, and family income < ₹6 Lakhs).

Who conducts the MP NEET MBBS Counselling 2026?

The counselling is conducted by the Department of Medical Education (DME), Madhya Pradesh through the official portal dme.mponline.gov.in.

Is NRI Quota available in MP Medical Colleges?

Yes, NRI quota seats are available in Private Medical Colleges in MP. The annual fees for NRI quota range from ₹20 Lakhs to ₹34 Lakhs depending on the college.

Do other state students get admission in MP Private Medical Colleges?

Other state students can apply, but they are considered only in the mop-up round or stray vacancy round after all eligible MP domicile candidates have been allotted seats. It is generally difficult for other state students to get seats in MP unless seats remain vacant.

What is the annual fee for MP Government Medical Colleges?

The annual fee for all Government Medical Colleges in MP is generally ₹1,14,000, which includes ₹1,00,000 tuition fee and ₹14,000 refundable caution money.
