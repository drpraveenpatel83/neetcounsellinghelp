⚡ How AIQ Counselling Works

1️⃣
Register Online
Register on MCC / AACCC portal and pay the counselling fee
2️⃣
Verify Documents
Online document verification — originals required at college reporting
3️⃣
Fill Choices
Fill preferred colleges and courses in rank order, then lock choices
4️⃣
Seat Allotment
NEET rank + choices + availability ke basis pe allotment
5️⃣
Report to College
Submit original documents and pay admission fee to confirm your seat
