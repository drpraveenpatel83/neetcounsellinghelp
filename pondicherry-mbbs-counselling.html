📋 Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Online Application
    Register on the CENTAC portal, fill in all required details, upload documents, and pay the application fee.
  • 2
    Draft & Final Merit List
    CENTAC publishes a draft merit list based on NEET scores. Candidates can raise objections if any, after which the Final Merit List is released.
  • 3
    Choice Filling
    Eligible candidates submit their preferred colleges and quotas.
  • 4
    Seat Allotment & Admission
    Download the provisional allotment order. Report physically to the allotted college for document verification and fee payment.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Pondicherry an Open State for NEET Counselling?

Yes, Pondicherry is an Open State. While the Government Quota seats are strictly reserved for UT domicile candidates, non-domicile candidates from other states can apply for the Management Quota seats in Private Medical Colleges.

Who conducts the Pondicherry MBBS Counselling 2026?

The counselling is conducted by the Centralised Admission Committee (CENTAC), Government of Puducherry.

What is the fee for IGMC&RI in Pondicherry?

For Indira Gandhi Medical College & Research Institute (IGMC&RI), the tuition fee is NIL, but the total 1st-year fee including admission, academic, lab, and refundable caution deposits is ₹1,43,700.
