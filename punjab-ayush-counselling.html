Candidates must pay a non-refundable registration fee through the Payment Gateway using Debit Card/Credit Card/Net Banking.

⚠️ Security Advance: At the time of admission, each student will have to pay a Security Advance of ₹25,000/- (refundable) along with the course fee.

📋 Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Online Registration
    NEET-2026 qualified candidates must visit www.graupunjab.org and fill out the online registration form carefully. A unique Registration ID and password will be sent via SMS/Email.
  • 2
    Fee Payment & Application Details
    Pay the requisite fee using the Payment Gateway. Fill out all particulars including Name, Preference of Course/College, Category, and NEET Marks.
  • 3
    Choice Filling (Institutional Preferences)
    Fill and submit your institutional preferences for getting seat allotment in the rounds of counselling.
  • 4
    Seat Allotment & Verification
    Seats are allotted as per the merit list and filled preferences. Report to the respective college with original credentials for eligibility checking, document verification, and medical checkup.
  • 5
    Fee Deposition
    After verification, deposit the fee in the shape of a Demand Draft from a nationalized bank in favor of "Registrar, Guru Ravidas Ayurved University" payable at Hoshiarpur.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who conducts the Punjab AYUSH Counselling?

Guru Ravidas Ayurved University (GRAU), Punjab conducts the counselling for all Government and Private AYUSH Colleges (BAMS, BHMS, BUMS) in the state.

What is the registration fee for Punjab AYUSH counselling?

The registration fee for State Quota Candidates is ₹4,600 (₹3,750 for SC candidates) and for All India Quota candidates it is ₹2,000.

What is the fee for Govt BAMS Colleges in Punjab?

The annual tuition fee for Government BAMS colleges in Punjab (like Govt. Ayurvedic College, Patiala) is approximately ₹82,500.
