✅ Eligibility & Domicile Rules

Punjab has specific domicile rules, with exemptions for certain categories like Defence Personnel.

Seat CategoryEligibility Criteria
85% Govt State QuotaCandidate must be a bonafide resident of Punjab OR have passed 10th and 12th from Punjab. Exemption applies for wards of Defence/Central Govt employees posted in Punjab.
Govt Quota in Pvt Colleges (50%)Strictly reserved for Punjab Domicile candidates.
Management Quota (35%)Open to all India. Non-domicile candidates can apply, but preference in some colleges is given to Punjab candidates first.
NRI Quota (15%)Open to NRI candidates globally.

📋 Punjab Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Online Registration & Fee Payment
    Candidates must register online on the BFUHS admission portal and pay the non-refundable registration fee (approx ₹5,900 for General).
  • 2
    Document Verification (For Reserved Categories)
    Candidates claiming NRI, Minority, or special state reservations must undergo physical/online document verification before the merit list.
  • 3
    State Merit List
    BFUHS will release the combined provisional merit list of all eligible registered candidates.
  • 4
    Choice Filling
    Candidates must log in to submit their preferences of colleges and courses.
  • 5
    Allotment & Fee Deposition
    Upon seat allotment, candidates must deposit the 6-month tuition fee (demand draft/online) to the BFUHS Registrar to confirm the seat, followed by college reporting.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Punjab an Open State for NEET Counselling?

Yes, Punjab is considered an Open State for Management Quota seats in Private Medical Colleges. Non-domicile candidates can apply, although some colleges may prioritize Punjab-domicile candidates first before opening seats to other states.

Who conducts the Punjab MBBS Counselling 2026?

The counselling is conducted by Baba Farid University of Health Sciences (BFUHS), Faridkot.

What is the fee for Government Medical Colleges in Punjab?

The tuition fee for Govt Medical Colleges in Punjab is incremental. It starts at ₹1,74,000 for the 1st year and increases annually, reaching a total of approx ₹6,79,000 for the full 5-year course.
