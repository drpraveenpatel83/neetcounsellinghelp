✅ Eligibility Criteria

CourseCriteria Notes
BAMS, BHMS & BUMS10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB). Min 50% for Gen, 40% for SC/ST/OBC. NEET-2026 qualification is mandatory.
BNYS10+2 (PCB) with English. NEET-2026 qualification is NOT mandatory, but NEET-qualified candidates will be given first preference.
Age LimitMinimum Age: 17 years (as of 31st December 2026). No upper age limit.

📋 Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Online Registration & Fee Payment
    Candidates must register online and pay the Registration Fee: ₹2,000 for General/OBC/EWS/Other states, and ₹1,500 for SC/ST of Rajasthan.
  • 2
    Merit List
    After registration, a state merit list is released based on NEET-2026 scores.
  • 3
    Choice Filling & Locking
    Candidates must log in to fill in their preferred choices of colleges and courses (BAMS/BHMS/BUMS/BNYS) based on their eligibility.
  • 4
    Seat Allotment & College Reporting
    Seats are allotted based on NEET scores and reservations. Candidates must report to the allotted college for document verification and admission fee payment.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who conducts the Rajasthan AYUSH Counselling 2026?

The UG/PG AYUSH Counselling Board, Rajasthan (under DSRRAU Jodhpur) conducts the counselling for 85% state quota and private AYUSH seats.

Is NEET mandatory for BNYS in Rajasthan?

NEET 2026 is NOT strictly mandatory for BNYS (Naturopathy & Yogic Science), but NEET-qualified candidates will be given first preference during seat allotment.

What is the registration fee for Rajasthan AYUSH Counselling?

The online registration fee is ₹2,000 for General/OBC/EWS and other state candidates. It is ₹1,500 for SC/ST candidates of Rajasthan.
