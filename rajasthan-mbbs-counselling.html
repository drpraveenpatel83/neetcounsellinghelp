📋 Counselling Process

Rajasthan NEET UG 2026 Counselling Procedure

Rajasthan NEET UG 2026 Counselling

Managed by: SMS Medical College, Jaipur (85% State Quota)

1

Registration

Candidates must apply online through the official portal. Fill in personal, academic, and NEET details. Application fees must be paid at this stage to complete the process.

2

Merit List & Document Verification

After registration, a state merit list is published. Shortlisted candidates must appear for offline document verification at designated centres with all original documents.

3

Choice Filling & Locking

Verified candidates can fill their preferred colleges and courses. Remember: Choices cannot be modified once they are locked.

4

Seat Allotment Rounds

Counselling includes Round 1, Round 2, Mop-Up, and Stray Vacancy rounds. Allotment is based on NEET score, reservation category, and college preferences.

5

Reporting to College

Allotted candidates must report physically to the college. Submit original documents and pay admission fees to confirm the seat.
