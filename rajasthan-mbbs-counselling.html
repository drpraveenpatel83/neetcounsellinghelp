📋 Counselling Process

  • 1
    Registration
    Candidates must register online on the official portal. Fill personal, academic, and NEET details. Application fee must be paid at this stage to complete registration.
  • 2
    Merit List & Document Verification
    After registration, state merit list is published. Shortlisted candidates must appear for offline document verification at designated centres with all original documents.
  • 3
    Choice Filling & Locking
    Verified candidates can fill preferred colleges and courses online. Choices cannot be modified once locked — fill carefully.
  • 4
    Seat Allotment Rounds
    Counselling includes Round 1, Round 2, Mop-Up, and Stray Vacancy rounds. Allotment is based on NEET score, reservation category, and college preferences.
  • 5
    Reporting to College
    Allotted candidates must report physically to the college, submit original documents, and pay admission fees to confirm the seat.
