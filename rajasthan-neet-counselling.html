📋 Counselling Process — Step by Step

  • 1
    NEET UG Result & Score Card
    NTA dwara NEET result declare hone ke baad score card download karo — yeh counselling ke liye mandatory document hai.
  • 2
    RUHS Portal par Registration
    ruhs.ac.in par online registration karo. Fee pay karo: General ₹2,000 | SC/ST ₹1,000. Rajasthan domicile certificate required hai.
  • 3
    Document Verification
    Original documents verification ke liye designated centre par jaana hoga. Yeh step physically complete karna padega.
  • 4
    Choice Filling & Locking
    Preferred colleges ko priority order mein fill karo. Choice lock hone ke baad change nahi hoti — carefully fill karo.
  • 5
    Seat Allotment — Round 1
    NEET rank, category aur choices ke basis par seat allot hogi. Result RUHS portal par publish hoga.
  • 6
    Reporting & Fee Payment
    Allotted college mein report karo aur admission fee pay karo — nahi kiya toh seat cancel ho sakti hai.
  • 7
    Round 2 & Mop-up Round
    Better college ke liye ya vacant seats ke liye Round 2 aur Mop-up round mein participate karo.

📋 Counselling Process — AYUSH

  • 1
    NEET UG Score Card
    MBBS jaisi hi requirement — NEET UG score card AYUSH admissions ke liye bhi mandatory hai.
  • 2
    RUHS AYUSH Counselling Registration
    RUHS portal par AYUSH counselling ke liye separate registration. BAMS, BHMS, BUMS — sab ke liye choices fill kar sakte ho.
  • 3
    Document Verification
    NEET result, 10th/12th certificates, domicile, aur category certificate original mein verify honge.
  • 4
    Choice Filling & Seat Allotment
    Preferred AYUSH colleges choose karo. NEET rank ke basis par seat allotment hogi.
  • 5
    College Joining
    Allotted college mein report karo aur admission fees pay karo.
