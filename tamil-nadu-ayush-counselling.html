✅ Eligibility Criteria & Domicile Rules

CriteriaDetails

📋 Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Application & Registration
    Fill the online application form on tnhealth.tn.gov.in and download the filled application. Some rounds may require sending hard copies.
  • 2
    Merit List
    The Selection Committee will publish the State Rank List based on the verified NEET UG scores and nativity.
  • 3
    Counselling & Choice Filling
    Eligible candidates must participate in the counselling process (online/offline as notified) and submit their preferences.
  • 4
    Seat Allotment
    Seats are allotted based on State Rank, Reservation Rule, and preferences submitted.
  • 5
    Reporting & Admission
    Download the allotment order, report to the allotted college within the stipulated time, and pay the tuition fee.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who conducts the Tamil Nadu AYUSH Counselling?

The Selection Committee, Directorate of Indian Medicine and Homoeopathy, Chennai conducts the Tamil Nadu state counselling for BSMS, BAMS, BUMS, and BHMS.

What is the fee for Private BAMS/BSMS Colleges in Tamil Nadu?

Under the Government Quota, the fee is ₹1,25,000 per annum. Under the Management Quota, the fee is ₹2,50,000 per annum for Self Financing Colleges.

Are non-Tamil Nadu students eligible for TN AYUSH counselling?

Yes, Tamil Nadu allows candidates from other states to participate through the 15% All India Quota (AIQ) in Self-Financing Institutions and Management Quota.
