📋 Counselling Process

  • 1
    Online Registration
    Register on tnmedicalselection.net. Note: Tamil Nadu conducts separate applications for Govt Quota (85% state) and Management Quota seats. Non-domiciles can apply for Management Quota.
  • 2
    Application Fee Payment
    Pay the non-refundable application fee (₹500 for Govt Quota; ₹1,000 for Management Quota). SC/SCA/ST are exempted from Govt Quota fee.
  • 3
    Merit List
    DMER TN publishes the State Merit List. Only candidates in this list are eligible for choice filling.
  • 4
    Choice Filling & Locking
    Pay the refundable security deposit to participate in choice filling. Select colleges and courses in order of preference.
  • 5
    Seat Allotment & Reporting
    Download allotment letter. Report to the allotted medical college with original documents and tuition fees.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Tamil Nadu an Open State for NEET Counselling?

Yes, Tamil Nadu is an Open State. Non-domicile candidates from other states can apply for the Management Quota and NRI Quota seats in Private Medical Colleges. However, Govt quota seats in Govt and Private colleges are strictly for TN domiciles.

Who conducts the Tamil Nadu MBBS Counselling 2026?

The counselling is conducted by the Directorate of Medical Education and Research (DMER), Tamil Nadu through the official portal tnmedicalselection.net.

What is the 7.5% Govt School Quota in Tamil Nadu?

Tamil Nadu has a special 7.5% horizontal reservation for students who have studied from Class 6 to 12 in Tamil Nadu state government schools. The cutoff for this quota is significantly lower, and the state bears the educational expenses for these students.

What is the annual fee for Government Medical Colleges in Tamil Nadu?

The annual fee for pure Government Medical Colleges in Tamil Nadu is approximately ₹13,610 per year (which includes a ₹4,000 tuition fee).
