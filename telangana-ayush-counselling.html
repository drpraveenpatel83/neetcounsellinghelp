Step-by-Step Process

  • 1
    Fill online application
    Register on the Telangana AYUSH portal and upload the required certificates in the prescribed format.
  • 2
    Check merit list
    Verify that your name, NEET rank and category details are correctly reflected before web options open.
  • 3
    Exercise web options
    Fill and lock your BAMS, BHMS, BUMS and BNYS choices in genuine preference order.
  • 4
    Download allotment letter
    Track allotment rounds and save the allotment order as soon as a seat is assigned.
  • 5
    Report to the allotted college
    Carry originals, pay the required admission fee and complete reporting within the deadline.
