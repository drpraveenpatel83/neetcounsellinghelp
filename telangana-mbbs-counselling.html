✅ Eligibility & Seat Categories

Telangana classifies private medical college seats into three distinct categories.

Seat CategoryEligibility Criteria
Category A (Competent Authority Quota)Strictly for Telangana/AP local Domicile candidates.
Category B (Management Quota)Open to all India. Non-domicile candidates from any state can apply.
Category C (NRI Quota)Open to NRI/NRI sponsored candidates globally.

📋 TS Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Online Registration & Fee Payment
    Register on the KNRUHS portal and pay the non-refundable registration/processing fee of ₹5,000. Candidates applying for Cat A and Cat B must submit separate applications if required by the notification.
  • 2
    Document Verification & Merit List
    The university verifies uploaded documents online and releases a Provisional Final Merit List. Only candidates in this list can fill choices.
  • 3
    Web Options Entry
    Candidates must exercise web options (Choice Filling) selecting their preferred colleges. There is no limit to the number of choices.
  • 4
    Seat Allotment & University Fee
    Upon seat allotment, candidates must pay the requisite "University Fee" (via Demand Draft or online) to download the final allotment letter.
  • 5
    Reporting to the College
    Report physically to the allotted medical college, submit original certificates, provide bank guarantee if asked, and pay the respective tuition fee.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Telangana an Open State for NEET Counselling?

Yes, Telangana is an Open State. While Category A (Govt Quota) seats are strictly for local domiciles, candidates from any state across India can apply for Category B (Management Quota) and Category C (NRI Quota) seats in Private Medical Colleges.

What are Category A, B, and C seats in Telangana?

Category A: Competent Authority Quota seats in private colleges with subsidized fees (~₹60,000/yr) reserved for local students.
Category B: Management Quota seats (~₹11L to ₹14L/yr) open to all India students.
Category C: NRI Quota seats.

Who conducts the Telangana MBBS Counselling 2026?

The counselling is conducted by Kaloji Narayana Rao University of Health Sciences (KNRUHS), Warangal.
