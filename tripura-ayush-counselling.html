Tripura AYUSH Counselling 2026

Complete guide for Tripura State AYUSH admissions. Check recent Government BAMS & BHMS college establishments, state-nominated seat criteria, and Directorate registration updates.

🌿 DME Tripura 📅 Updated: April 2026 🎯 Upgraded Seat Pool

Tripura AYUSH Counselling is governed by the Directorate of Medical Education (DME), Tripura. Historically, students utilized AACCC quotas to attend colleges outside the state. Starting recently in the 2026-27 framework, Tripura formally established its first Government Ayurveda and Homeopathy Medical Colleges. These colleges bring new state quotas specifically for genuine residents of Tripura, supplementing traditional state-sponsored seats hosted in external institutions.

📋 Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Online Application
    Register on dme.tripura.gov.in using verified NEET Roll Numbers when the AYUSH notification goes live.
  • 2
    Preparation of State Merit List
    A localized merit hierarchy is broadcasted containing approved domicile candidates categorised by ST, SC, and UR.
  • 3
    Physical / Online Choice Filling
    Input preferences selecting between within-state or external state-sponsored seats.
  • 4
    Seat Allotment Distribution
    Check the official PDF allocations mapping your seat with the proper institutional verification.
  • 5
    Document Inspection & Payment
    Carry authentic IDs and educational transcripts for reporting against the baseline governmental fees (tentatively ~₹20,000 yearly).

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who conducts the Tripura AYUSH Counselling 2026?

The Directorate of Medical Education (DME), Tripura regulates the state-level processing.

Does Tripura have its own BAMS and BHMS colleges?

Yes, as of recently, the state has actively pushed establishing its own dedicated Ayurveda and Homeopathy medical hospitals offering 60 internal seats apiece!

Can non-domiciles apply for Tripura State AYUSH seats?

Non-domiciles lack access to the 85% localized state quotas; however, depending on the status of AACCC registration, 15% AIQ seats would be processed uniformly across states.
