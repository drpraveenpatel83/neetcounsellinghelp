📋 Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Online Registration & Fee Payment
    Candidates must register online on the DME Tripura admission portal and pay the requisite counselling registration fee.
  • 2
    State Merit List
    DME Tripura will publish a provisional state merit list of all eligible registered candidates based on their NEET UG score and state domicile.
  • 3
    Choice Filling & Locking
    Log in to submit and arrange your preferences for medical/dental colleges. Lock the choices before the deadline.
  • 4
    Allotment & College Reporting
    Download the provisional allotment letter and report to the designated college for document verification, medical checkup, and fee payment.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Tripura an Open State for NEET Counselling?

Yes, Tripura is an Open State. While 85% of Government college seats are strictly reserved for Tripura domicile students, candidates from any state in India can participate in the counselling to secure admission in the Management Quota seats of Private Medical Colleges.

Who conducts the Tripura MBBS Counselling 2026?

The counselling is conducted by the Directorate of Medical Education (DME), Tripura.

What is the fee for Tripura Medical College?

The annual tuition fee for Tripura Medical College and Dr. B R A M Teaching Hospital, Agartala is ₹13,50,000 per year.
