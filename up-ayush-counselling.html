📋 Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Online Registration & Fee Payment
    Register on the official portal. Pay the non-refundable registration fee. A one-time registration is sufficient for all normal rounds.
  • 2
    Security Amount Deposit
    Pay the required refundable security deposit online (Govt: ₹20k, Pvt/Both: ₹50k) to activate choice filling.
  • 3
    State Merit List & Choice Filling
    A merit list is published. Eligible candidates then select and lock their preferred colleges and courses.
  • 4
    Document Verification at Nodal Centre
    After tentative allotment, physical document verification occurs at designated nodal centers. Failure to verify leads to disqualification.
  • 5
    Final Allotment & Admission
    Download final allotment letter and report to the designated college. Admitted candidates can opt for upgradation in Round 2.

🔄 Round-wise Counselling Rules

Understand the specific conditions for each round.

  • Round 1: Admission to allotted seat is mandatory. You can opt for upgradation using an OTP-based consent. If you don't take admission, security deposit is forfeited.
  • Round 2: New registrations allowed. Admitted candidates from R1 can opt for re-allocation (do not select the same seat). Security deposit is forfeited if new allotted seat is not taken.
  • Round 3: Resignations from earlier rounds must be submitted 48 hours before R3 choice filling to get a refund (minus ₹1000 admission fee). Non-allottees can participate without redepositing security.
  • Stray Vacancy: Mandatory physical verification. Re-registration is required (Existing candidates pay ₹1,000; new candidates pay ₹2,000). No seat reshuffling allowed.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who conducts the UP AYUSH Counselling 2026?

The Department of AYUSH, Government of Uttar Pradesh conducts the counselling for BAMS, BHMS, and BUMS seats via the official portal upayushcounseling.upsdc.gov.in.

What is the security deposit for UP Private AYUSH Colleges?

To participate in the counselling for Private AYUSH Colleges in UP, candidates must pay a refundable security deposit of ₹50,000. For Government colleges, the deposit is ₹20,000.

What is the fee for Govt BAMS Colleges in UP?

The total 1st year fee for Govt AYUSH colleges is ₹19,000 (which includes ₹10,000 Tuition Fee, ₹4,000 Caution Money, plus other fees). For reserved categories, the tuition fee is discounted by 50%, making the total 1st-year fee ₹14,000.
