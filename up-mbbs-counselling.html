📋 Counselling Process

  • 1
    Online Registration
    Register on upneet.gov.in using NEET UG Roll No. and Email ID. Pay the non-refundable registration fee of ₹2,000.
  • 2
    Payment of Security Deposit
    After registration, candidates must pay the security money (Govt: ₹30k, Pvt: ₹3 Lakhs) via online mode to be eligible for choice filling.
  • 3
    Online Document Verification
    UP conducts online verification of documents. A state merit list is released for candidates whose documents are successfully verified.
  • 4
    Choice Filling & Locking
    Login to the portal and arrange medical colleges in order of preference. Remember to save and lock choices before the deadline.
  • 5
    Seat Allotment & Nodal Centre Reporting
    Download allotment letter. Report physically to the assigned Nodal Centre / Medical College for final admission and fee submission.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Uttar Pradesh an Open State for NEET Counselling?

Yes, Uttar Pradesh is an Open State. Non-domicile students from other states can apply for the 100% seats in Private Medical and Dental Colleges. However, Government college seats are strictly reserved for UP domicile candidates only.

What is the security deposit for UP Private Medical Colleges?

Candidates opting for Private MBBS colleges in UP must deposit a one-time refundable security amount of ₹3,00,000. For government colleges, the deposit is ₹30,000, and for BDS it is ₹1,00,000.

Who conducts the UP NEET MBBS Counselling 2026?

The counselling is conducted by the Directorate General of Medical Education and Training (DGME), Uttar Pradesh through the official portal upneet.gov.in.

What happens if I don't join the allotted seat in Round 2?

If a candidate is allotted a seat in Round 2 and fails to join the allotted college, their security deposit will be forfeited (₹3 Lakhs for Pvt MBBS). They must register fresh for the Mop-up round if they wish to continue.
