📋 Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Online Registration
    Register yourself on the UAU portal (uau.ac.in) by providing NEET UG details and personal information.
  • 2
    Fee Payment
    Pay the non-refundable ₹2,000 registration fee online to activate your application.
  • 3
    Choice Filling & Locking
    Log in and fill your preferred choices of AYUSH colleges. Save and lock choices before the deadline.
  • 4
    Seat Allotment & Document Verification
    Download the provisional allotment letter. Report to the allotted college for document verification and admission confirmation.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who conducts the Uttarakhand AYUSH Counselling 2026?

The Uttarakhand Ayurved University (UAU), Dehradun conducts the AYUSH counselling for BAMS, BHMS, and BUMS seats via their official website uau.ac.in.

Is Uttarakhand an Open State for AYUSH Counselling?

Yes, non-domicile candidates from other states can participate and get admission into the Management/All India Quota seats in Private AYUSH Colleges in Uttarakhand.

What is the fee for Govt BAMS Colleges in Uttarakhand?

The annual fee for Govt BAMS colleges is approximately ₹1,50,000/- to ₹1,78,400/- per year depending on the campus (Rishikul vs Gurukul).
