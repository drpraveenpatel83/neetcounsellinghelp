Step-by-Step Process

  • 1
    Register on the state portal
    Create your login and enter NEET details, category details and contact information.
  • 2
    Upload documents
    Keep domicile and reservation documents ready if you are applying under the state route.
  • 3
    Track merit and seat matrix
    Check the official university notice board before choice filling opens.
  • 4
    Fill choices carefully
    Uttarakhand has a wide range of private AYUSH options, so fee strategy matters as much as rank strategy.
  • 5
    Report after allotment
    Download the allotment letter, report on time and complete fee payment plus document verification.
