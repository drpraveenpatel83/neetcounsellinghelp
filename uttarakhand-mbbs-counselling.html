📋 Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Online Registration & Fee Payment
    Candidates must register online on the HNBUMU portal and pay the required non-refundable counselling registration fee.
  • 2
    Security Deposit
    Pay the refundable security deposit online depending on the type of seat (Govt or Private) to participate in choice filling.
  • 3
    State Merit List
    HNBUMU will publish a provisional state merit list of all eligible registered candidates based on their NEET UG score.
  • 4
    Choice Filling & Locking
    Log in to submit and lock your preferences for medical/dental colleges in Uttarakhand.
  • 5
    Allotment & College Reporting
    Download the provisional allotment letter and report to the designated college/nodal center for document verification, medical checkup, and fee payment.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Uttarakhand an Open State for NEET Counselling?

Yes, Uttarakhand is an Open State. While 85% of Government college seats are strictly reserved for Uttarakhand domicile students, candidates from any state in India can participate in the counselling to secure admission in the Management Quota seats of Private Medical Colleges.

Who conducts the Uttarakhand MBBS Counselling 2026?

The counselling is conducted by Hemwati Nandan Bahuguna Uttarakhand Medical Education University (HNBUMU), Dehradun.

What is the Bond Policy in Uttarakhand Govt Medical Colleges?

Uttarakhand has a unique fee structure for Govt medical colleges. Candidates willing to serve a rural service bond for a few years are charged a highly subsidized fee (approx ₹1.45 Lakh/year). Candidates opting out of the bond pay a significantly higher fee (approx ₹4 Lakh/year).
