VCI NEET UG Counselling 2026 (15% AIQ BVSc & AH)

Complete guide for 15% All India Quota in recognized Veterinary Colleges. Check VCI registration, stray round fees, and process.

🐾 VCI (Veterinary Council of India) 📅 Updated: April 2026 🎯 All India Open

Important: The Veterinary Council of India (VCI) conducts counselling for 15% All India Quota seats for admission to recognized Veterinary Colleges (BVSc & AH degree course) across India. The remaining 85% state quota seats are counselled by individual state authorities.

📋 Counselling Process (Step-by-Step)

  • 1
    Online Registration & Fee Payment
    Register on the official portal using NEET credentials. Pay the non-refundable registration fee.
  • 2
    Choice Filling & Locking
    Select preferences of veterinary colleges. Arrange them in order of priority and lock the choices before the deadline. [6]
  • 3
    Seat Allotment Result
    VCI allocates seats based on NEET rank, reservation rules, and submitted choices. [2]
  • 4
    Reporting & Upgradation
    Report to the allotted college. If a candidate is allotted a seat in Round 1 but doesn't join, they cannot participate in further rounds unless specific fresh registration rules apply (as per the year's bulletin). [1]
  • 5
    Mop-Up & Stray Vacancy Round
    Fresh registrations are usually allowed in the Mop-Up round. For Stray Vacancy, existing registered candidates must pay a high participation fee (security deposit) to participate. [1]

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who conducts the VCI counselling?

The Veterinary Council of India (VCI) conducts the 15% All India Quota counselling for BVSc & AH courses. [1]

What is the VCI registration fee?

The non-refundable registration fee is ₹1000 for General/OBC/EWS candidates and ₹500 for SC/ST/PwD candidates. [1]

Is there a security deposit in VCI counselling?

Yes, specifically for the Stray Vacancy Round. A mandatory participation fee (security deposit) of ₹50,000 for General/OBC/EWS and ₹25,000 for SC/ST/PwD must be paid to participate in the stray round. This is refunded if no seat is allotted, but forfeited if an allotted seat is not joined. [1]

Are State Quota seats also counselled by VCI?

No, VCI only counsels for the 15% All India Quota. The remaining 85% of veterinary seats are counselled by the respective state authorities.
