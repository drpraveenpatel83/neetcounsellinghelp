✅ Eligibility, Nativity & Domicile Rules

CategoryCriteria Notes
85% State QuotaCandidate must qualify NEET UG 2026 and satisfy West Bengal domicile / nativity conditions through accepted documentary proof.
Proforma A1For candidates who have been residing in West Bengal continuously for at least 10 years.
Proforma A2For candidates who have lived in West Bengal continuously for 10 years and also passed 10+2 from the state.
Proforma BFor candidates whose parent is a permanent resident of West Bengal and has continuous residency in the state for at least the last 10 years.
Private / Management QuotaNEET-qualified candidates may participate for applicable private management quota seats even without West Bengal domicile, subject to that year’s brochure and verification rules.
Basic QualificationMust qualify NEET UG 2026, be at least 17 years old by 31 December 2026, and pass PCB + English in Class 12.
📌 If parent-based domicile is used, candidates should keep any two parent identity proofs such as Aadhaar, Voter ID, or Passport showing a West Bengal address for verification.

📋 Step-by-Step WBMCC Counselling Process

  • 1
    Online Registration & Fee Payment
    Visit wbmcc.nic.in, complete the online registration, verify NEET details, and pay the counselling fee according to your category.
  • 2
    Physical Verification at Designated College
    Candidates must report physically at the designated verification centre with original documents, domicile papers, and category certificates. NRI instructions are usually issued separately.
  • 3
    Verified Candidate List & Seat Matrix
    Only successfully verified candidates become eligible for online choice filling after the publication of the verified list and round-wise seat matrix.
  • 4
    Choice Filling & Locking
    Fill and lock your preferred colleges and quotas carefully. West Bengal allotment depends on NEET rank, reservation, verification status, and the order of choices.
  • 5
    Seat Allotment & Reporting
    Download the allotment letter, report to the allotted reporting centre / institute with original documents, complete re-verification, and pay the required college fee.
  • 6
    Upgradation / Later Rounds
    If participating in later rounds, follow WBMCC notices regarding fresh registration, seat surrender, willingness, and upgraded allotment rules.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who conducts West Bengal MBBS Counselling 2026?

West Bengal MBBS counselling is conducted by the West Bengal Medical Counselling Committee (WBMCC) under the Department of Health & Family Welfare, Government of West Bengal.

Is West Bengal an open state for MBBS counselling?

For the 85% state quota, West Bengal is treated as a domicile-based state and candidates must satisfy the nativity rules. For certain private management quota seats, NEET-qualified non-domicile candidates may also participate as per the current year brochure.

What domicile forms are used in West Bengal counselling?

West Bengal counselling commonly uses Proforma A1, A2 and B depending on whether the candidate is claiming domicile on personal residence, schooling in the state, or parent-based permanent residence.

What is the counselling registration fee in West Bengal?

The counselling registration fee is commonly shown as ₹2,000 for Unreserved / OBC and ₹1,500 for SC / ST candidates. Candidates must confirm the latest brochure before payment.
